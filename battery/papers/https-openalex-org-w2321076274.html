Twin Problems of Interfacial Carbonate Formation in Nonaqueous Li–O<sub>2</sub> Batteries

Year: 2012  DOI ↗

Main topic: battery technology

Abstract
We use XPS and isotope labeling coupled with differential electrochemical mass spectrometry (DEMS) to show that small amounts of carbonates formed during discharge and charge of Li-O2 cells in ether electrolytes originate from reaction of Li2O2 (or LiO2) both with the electrolyte and with the C cathode.
