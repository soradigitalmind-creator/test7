Lithium Bond Chemistry in Lithium–Sulfur Batteries

Year: 2017  DOI ↗

Main topic: sulfur battery

Abstract
The lithium-sulfur (Li-S) battery is a promising high-energy-density storage system. The strong anchoring of intermediates is widely accepted to retard the shuttle of polysulfides in a working battery. However, the understanding of the intrinsic chemistry is still deficient.
